Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is a sauce from southern Mexico. In addition to avocado, the ingredients are often tomato, onion and/or garlic, juice of a lime or lemon, chili pepper, coriander leaves and various other herbs.

Ingredients

    3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed

    1 lime, juiced

    1 teaspoon salt

    Â½ cup diced onion

    3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

    2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced

    1 teaspoon minced garlic

    1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (Optional)

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt

  2. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic.

  3. Stir in cayenne pepper.

  4. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.
